Anagram Master

Will you be the next Anagram Master?

Each game consists of 8 rounds. In each of them you are presented with an anagram related to the theme you have selected and have to find the right word.

The time you have to find the word in each round depend on the difficulty level you select. In the Normal level you have 45 seconds to solve the anagram and in the hard 25.

Every correct answer gives you 3 points.

You can ask for a hint in each word, but doing so will deduct 1 point from your score in the normal level and 2 points in the hard level.
